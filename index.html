Welcome to the next level of RC vehicles. Blue Thunder is a remote-controlled hovercraft, equipped with a 720p waterproof camera, letting you shoot all your adventures in high-definition. It offers unparalled maneuverability, even at speeds as high as 60 km/h. It can navigate all kinds of terrain, from super smooth hardwood floors, to forests, fields, rivers, and lakes.

What started off as a university project (show in video) has now evolved into something much bigger. It's power comes from 2 4800mAh battery packs, which are used to power the three ducted fans. One fan is used for lift, while the other two are used for thrust. These are implemented using a thrust vectoring system, similar to those used in jet planes.

FAQ

Are the pictures and videos showing the product as it will be shipped?
No, these are currently up to show the initial project serving as a basis for the final design
The hovercraft does not appear to be moving very fast, what gives?
Due to the size of the competition field, top speeds had to be reduced. As well, the production model will be constructed of lighter materials, allowing for a higher acceleration rate and top speed.
Will the site and FAQ be updated?
Yes, once the first official prototype has been completed
